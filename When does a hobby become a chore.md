*I want to read more, but who am I really doing it for?*

Every near year I sit down and set myself some new goals, and almost every year there is some goal to the effect of 'I want to do more of hobby x'. But why haven't I already spent time doing it if it's a hobby?

## Short Term Satisfaction
The TV is an ever present option, which is often a more immediate hit of endorphins than reading or learning a musical instrument, but it's also something I want to do less of.
