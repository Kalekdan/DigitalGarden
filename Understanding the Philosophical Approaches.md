**Epicurean** - Controlling life such that it is secure from loss (aiming to achieve close to enlightenment via simplifying life and desire, such that you are unaffected by the world around you)
**Stoicism** - Controlling your emotions and reactions to your circumstances such that you are able to react and respond to changes in situation
